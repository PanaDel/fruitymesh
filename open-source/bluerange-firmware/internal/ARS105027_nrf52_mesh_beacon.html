3rd Generation Beacon

MeshBeacon ARS105027 (boardid 16)

Mesh beacon ARS105027 comprises of several sensors and the Laird BL652-SA Module.

  • Pin Assignment Boardid 16: LED Red(7), LED Green(6).

  • Battery: 4x AA Battery (1000mAh to 3600mAh each).

  • Sensors: accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer, temperature and optical.
