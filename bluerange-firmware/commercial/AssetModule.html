AssetModule

General

AssetModule acquires measurement from accelerometer and barometer and use them to calculate velocity, pressure, temperature and humidity. It advertises asset packet at 100 ms when the asset is moving and 1000 ms when it is not moving.

Battery Measurements

Battery Measurement can be reproduced by flashing the asset beacon with prod_asset_nrf52 featureset. The battery measurement of asset beacon ARS104917 are as follows:

Asset Movement Scenarios Battery Measurement

Asset in non-moving state

22 - 24 uA

Asset in moving state

96 - 99 uA
